# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: May 29, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the **TabAlign** Chrome extension handles information.

## 1. No Data Collection
TabAlign does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. 

## 2. Permissions Used
The extension utilizes the following browser permissions solely to perform its core functions:
- **tabs**: Required to read the URLs of active tabs to parse domain names for grouping. This data is handled in-memory and is never stored.
- **tabGroups**: Required to create, name, color-code, and collapse tab groups in the active browser window.
- **storage**: Required to save your customized configuration preferences (such as minimum tab limits, ignored domains, and A/B theme states) locally on your device.

## 3. Data Storage
All configuration settings are saved locally inside your browser's sandboxed storage (`chrome.storage.local`). No information is uploaded to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.

## 4. No Analytics or Tracking
TabAlign does not contain any analytics software, tracking codes, or third-party tracking pixels.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please open an issue in the public GitHub repository.
